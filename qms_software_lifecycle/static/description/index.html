Professional Quality Management System (QMS)

Complete Software Lifecycle Traceability from Requirements to Release

A robust, structured, and auditable Quality Management System specifically designed for software development companies. Built for Odoo 19 Community Edition, this addon provides the documented evidence required for ISO 9001 and other quality standards.

Full Lifecycle Traceability

Requirement to Test

Link your business and functional requirements directly to test cases. Ensure that every requirement is verified and every test has a purpose.

Defect to Change

Track defects found during testing and link them to change requests. Maintain a clear history of why changes were made and how they were verified.

Risk to Mitigation

Identify technical and organizational risks early. Assign mitigation plans and track their execution to ensure project success.

Release Baselines

Create immutable baselines for your releases. Capture the exact state of requirements, specs, and tests at any point in time for audit purposes.

Professional Reporting & Insights

Interactive Analytics

Deeply analyze your quality data with professional Pivot and Graph views. Track test success rates, defect density, and project progress in real-time with sample data support.

Formal PDF Documentation

Generate high-quality, professional PDF reports with a single click: Requirement Specifications, Test Execution Reports, Risk Registers, and Release Notes.

Customer Collaboration (Portal)

Full Portal Interaction

Share QMS Projects with your customers and external partners just like standard Odoo projects. They can view, edit, and create requirements or defects directly from their portal account.

Real-time Communication

Integrated Chatter and message history allow for seamless discussion between your team and the customer, fully documented within the quality records.
